We at Real Bricks have immense expertise in this field and know what a good quality brick consists of. Some of the parameters of a good brick is mentioned below:

  1. Good thermal insulation

    Bricks have these amazing properties of maintaining the indoor temperature very well. If the weather is hot, cold or even rainy good bricks help in maintaining ambient temperature inside the building.

  2. Good compression strength

    Compression strength is what determines how strong your brick is and determines how much compression load it can take. A good compression strength means a sturdy building which will keep you and your family safe.

  3. Excellent Compression strength to weight ratio

    Another parameter which determines how robust your building will be. It’s very important to have excellent compression strength to weight ratio especially if you live in a natural calamity prone area for example earthquakes.

  4. Easy workability

    The easier the workability of the brick, faster will be the construction of the building.

  5. Great adhesive property with cement

    As you might have understood greater the adhesion the more foolproof will be the building to adverse weather conditions. It’s important to use good quality cement for the same too.
